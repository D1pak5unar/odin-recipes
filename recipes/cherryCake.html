This is a peanut chicken Recipe

Cherry Cake Made with care:

The Cherry Cake dish features tender ribs pieces coated in bbq sauce, garnished with fresh garnish It is served with coleslaw and vegetables like broccoli and bell peppers. A small side of pickled carrots and cucumbers adds a tangy contrast. The presentation is neat and visually appealing, enhancing the dish's appetizing appearance.

Ingredients:

For Chicken and sauce
  • Chicken titties and thighs
  • Peanut Butter (crunchy or creamy as you like)
  • Garlic
  • Salt peppers
  • Soy sauce
  • Lime Juice
  • Fresh Cilantro for garnish
  • Coconut Milk
  • Ginger Minced
  • Honey and Brown sugar
  • Red Pepper flakes
  • Peanuts for garnish
  • olive oil
For the sides
  1. Short grain rice
  2. Brocoli
  3. Bell peppers
  4. Carrots
  5. Cucumbers
  6. Vinegar
  7. Sugar
  8. Salt to your liking

Steps to Make Peanut Chicken with Short grain Rice and Vegetables:

  1. Chicken: Cut chicken breasts or thighs into bite-sized pieces. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Vegetables: Slice bell peppers, cut broccoli into florets, and julienne or thinly slice carrots and cucumbers.
  3. Pickling Solution: In a bowl, mix rice vinegar, a pinch of sugar, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Pickle Vegetables: Add carrots and cucumbers to the pickling solution and let them sit while you prepare the rest of the dish.
  5. Rinse Rice: Rinse jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  6. Cook Rice: Cook rice according to package instructions or in a rice cooker.
  7. Heat Oil: In a separate large pan, heat oil over medium-high heat. Cook Chicken: Add chicken pieces and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.
  8. Same Pan: In the same pan used for the chicken, add a little more oil if needed. Sauté: Add broccoli and bell peppers, sauté until tender but still crisp.
  9. Combine Chicken and Sauce: Add cooked chicken back to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the peanut sauce over the mixture and stir to coat evenly. Garnish: Garnish with chopped peanuts and fresh cilantro.
  10. Rice: Place a serving of jasmine rice on each plate. Chicken and Vegetables: Add the peanut chicken and vegetables next to the rice. Pickled Vegetables: Add a small portion of pickled carrots and cucumbers on the side.
  11. Serve the dish hot and enjoy your homemade peanut chicken with jasmine rice and vegetables.
